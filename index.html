Design in Everyday Life

Design influences our daily lives in profound yet often unnoticed ways, shaping how we interact with the world. This website offers a deep dive into three crucial aspects of ,art, function, and color-- highlighting their role in creativity and everyday life.

Through curated works by influential design thinkers, we explore how art, practicality, and visual aesthetics come together to create meaningful and engaging experiences.

Each text invites readers to reflect on the thoughtful decisions behind the objects and spaces we encounter every day. Together, they illuminate how design isn’t just about what we use, but how we live.
